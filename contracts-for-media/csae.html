General Commitment and Policy for All Apps

Our Commitment

Reports AI: Followers Tracker is published by Private Limited on Google Play and operated by 618Media. We are dedicated to protecting children and maintaining a secure digital environment across Reports AI and all our apps.

As part of our unwavering commitment, we enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy for any content or behavior related to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). CSAE and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) are strictly prohibited in Reports AI and all apps published by Private Limited.

This policy is in compliance with Google Play's Child Safety Standards and other global child protection regulations.

Scope of Policy

This CSAE Policy applies comprehensively to Reports AI: Followers Tracker and all apps published by Private Limited, including:

  • All Content and Features: This includes, but is not limited to, any text, images, audio, or video published or generated within our apps.
  • User-Generated Content (UGC): Any contributions made by users through feedback, support forms, messaging, or other interactive features where available within the app.
  • Behavior and Activity Monitoring: Activities performed by users, especially those involving interactions with minors, to ensure compliance with safety guidelines.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities and content are strictly prohibited across Reports AI and all apps published by Private Limited:

  • Distribution of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Any form of visual, textual, or audio depiction of minors engaged in sexually explicit conduct.
  • Child Grooming: Attempts to build a relationship with a minor online with the intention of exploiting them sexually or otherwise.
  • Sextortion: Blackmail or coercion involving intimate images of minors.
  • Sexualization of Minors: Any content that encourages or supports the inappropriate sexual depiction of children.
  • Facilitation of Child Trafficking: Any activity promoting or enabling the exploitation of minors for commercial or sexual purposes.

Preventive Measures and Actions Across Reports AI

Proactive Safety Measures

To maintain a safe and secure environment, Private Limited applies the following measures across Reports AI and our apps:

  • Age-Appropriate Design: Reports AI is not directed to children. We do not knowingly target children with our app or collect personal data from children.
  • Review Procedures: We review child safety reports and take action to remove inappropriate or harmful content swiftly.
  • In-App Reporting Tools: Reports AI provides report issue and feedback features, enabling users to flag inappropriate content or suspicious behavior quickly.
  • Regular Staff Training: Moderation staff undergo regular training to stay updated on the latest threats and compliance standards related to CSAE.
  • Collaboration with Authorities: We actively cooperate with child protection organizations and law enforcement to report and address any violations.

Response to Violations

If any CSAE-related content or activity is identified within our apps, we take immediate and decisive action, including:

  • Immediate Content Removal: Any content violating CSAE policies is removed from our platform as soon as it is detected or reported.
  • User Account Suspension/Termination: Users responsible for CSAE violations will have their accounts suspended or permanently terminated.
  • Legal Reporting: Confirmed incidents of CSAE are reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or appropriate authorities for investigation and legal action.
